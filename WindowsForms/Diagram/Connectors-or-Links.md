# Connectors or Links

Connectors and lines have the following decorators:

* Circle
* CircleCross
* CircleReverseArrow
* Cross45
* Cross90
* CrossReverseArrow
* Custom
* Diamond
* DimensionLine
* DoubleArrow
* DoubleCross
* Filled45Arrow
* Filled60Arrow
* FilledCircle
* FilledDiamond
* FilledFancyArrow
* FilledSquare
* None
* Open45Arrow
* Open60Arrow
* OpenFancyArrow
* ReverseArrow
* ReverseDoubleArrow
* Square
